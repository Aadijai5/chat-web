Frequently Asked Questions

What is ETHON?

ETHON is your ultimate hackathon companion app that helps you manage events, track progress, and collaborate with your team effectively.

Is ETHON free to use?

Yes, ETHON is completely free to download and use. We believe in supporting the hackathon community without any barriers.

How do I install ETHON?

Simply download the APK file, enable installation from unknown sources in your Android settings, and follow the installation prompts. Check our Installation Guide section for detailed steps.

Can I use ETHON offline?

Yes, most features of ETHON work offline. However, team collaboration and real-time updates require an internet connection.

How do I update ETHON?

ETHON will notify you when updates are available. Simply download and install the new version to keep your app up to date with the latest features and improvements.
